# Path Culture Book
## The 12 Principles of Path

---

## 1. Slack Is Our Communication Center

Slack is the official communication platform of Path.

All operational communication, project discussions, and decisions are conducted within the Path Slack Workspace.

While other tools may be used when necessary, Slack remains the single source of truth for communication.

---

## 2. We Work Remote-First

Path operates as a remote-first organization.

Where we work from is not important.  
What matters is the value we produce.

Working hours are organized based on:

- project needs  
- team coordination  
- delivery timelines  

---

## 3. Ownership Culture

Every task at Path has an owner.

The code we write, the systems we build, and the results we deliver are our responsibility.

Nothing is ever left with the mindset of:

> “My job is done. Someone else will handle the rest.”

---

## 4. We Are AI-First

Path is an AI-First organization.

We actively use AI tools for:

- research  
- development  
- documentation  
- testing  
- automation  
- decision support  

However, we always remember:

> AI generates. Humans are responsible.

---

## 5. The Rule of Three Checks

No work at Path is delivered without verification.

Every output must be checked at least three times:

1. Logical review  
2. Technical validation  
3. End-user impact review  

Quality is not accidental.  
It is the result of discipline.

---

## 6. We Understand the Problem Better Than the Sender

One of Path’s core mottos:

> We never deliver work without understanding the problem better than the person who sent it.

We do not produce superficial solutions.  
We aim to understand the root cause of the problem.

---

## 7. Documentation Is Culture

Knowledge at Path lives in systems, not in people's heads.

Every project should include:

- README documentation  
- technical explanations  
- architecture descriptions  

Documentation is not written after the work is done.  
It is written while the work is being done.

---

## 8. Simplicity Is Power

We build simple and sustainable systems.

Good engineering produces solutions that are:

- readable  
- understandable  
- maintainable  

Complexity is avoided whenever possible.

---

## 9. Automation Always Wins

Repeated work should never depend on humans.

At Path:

> If something can be automated, it should be automated.

CI/CD pipelines, scripts, AI agents, and automation tools are natural parts of our workflow.

---

## 10. Speed and Quality Go Together

Path moves fast.

But speed is never an alternative to quality.

Speed is achieved through:

- the right tools  
- the right automation  
- the right architecture  

---

## 11. Learning Never Stops

Technology constantly evolves.

Everyone at Path is expected to:

- learn  
- research  
- share knowledge  

Knowledge is never hidden.  
It is shared.

---

## 12. We Deliver Results

At Path we measure:

- not hours  
- not effort  

but results.

We do not produce tasks.  
We produce outcomes.

---

# Path Culture Summary

Path is:

- an AI-First organization
- a remote-first team
- a culture of ownership and responsibility
- an engineering environment driven by automation and learning

We do not just build software.

We build systems that create impact.
